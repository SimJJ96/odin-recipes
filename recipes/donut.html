Donut

A sweet,buttery and fluffy treat

Only using yeast would produce the airy and fluffy donuts.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Egg Yolks
  • Yeast
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Stir together warm water, sugar, and yeast and let rise for about 5 minutes.
  2. Mix together the rest of the sugar donut ingredients, including the proofed yeast. Itâ€™s best if you use a dough hook attachment to do this.
  3. Roll out the sugar donut dough and use a 3-inch cookie cutter to cut out donuts.
  4. Let rise again for about an hour, until doubled or tripled in volume.
  5. Heat your oil and fry the donuts. Once they are cool enough, roll them in sugar.
  6. Viola! Eat and enjoy your donuts!
