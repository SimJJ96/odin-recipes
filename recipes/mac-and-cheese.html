Mac and Cheese

A rich and creamy macaroni pasta mixed with a cheesy sauce

Adding milk to cheddar and parmesan cheese to produce the best cheese sauce in town.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter and flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni in salted water until the pasta are al dente. Drain and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Melt the butter, then whisk in the flour. Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in the cheeses. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering until the sauce is thick. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.
  3. Melt two tablespoons of butter in a skillet, add the bread crumbs, and toast until the crumbs are brown. Spread the topping over the macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the topping is golden brown.
