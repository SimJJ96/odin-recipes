Nutella Tarts

Fragrant buttery biscuit topped with sweet and creamy nutella topping

Bake the tart a second time to fix the Nutella.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup or 115g of Unsalted Butter, Softened at Room Temperature
  • 1 and 1/2 Cup or 180g of All-Purpose / Plain Flour (and extra for flouring the surface)
  • 2 Tablespoon or 35g of Granulated White Sugar
  • 2 Tablespoon or 20g of Milk Powder (Optional)
  • 1 Egg Yolk
  • 1/4 Tsp or 1.4g of Table Salt
  • 1 Tsp or 5ml Vanilla Essence
  • 300g of Nutella

Steps

  1. Cream together softened butter and sugar until it is light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
  2. Then, add in salt, egg and vanilla before mixing well.
  3. Sift the flour and milk powder.
  4. Next, fold in the dry ingredients into the mixture containing the well-mixed butter, sugar and egg yolk.
  5. Once completed, refrigerate the batter for 25 minutes. In the meantime, preheat the oven to 160 degrees C (or 325F).
  6. Then, flour a clean surface and roll out the dough until about 0.5cm thickness.
  7. Using a cookie cutter, cut out the dough and transfer to baking tray.
  8. Bake for about 12min 45 to 13min 45s or until it is starting to brown on the edges.
  9. Leave to cool for about 30 minutes.
  10. Then, pipe your Nutella filling over the cookie. These filling will not expand.
